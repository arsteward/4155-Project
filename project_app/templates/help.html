Help


List of allowed text and image formats



Text formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT

Image formats: PNG, JPEG

Instead of using the textbox, you can upload text and image files. After uploading a file, select from one of the three algorithms (listed below) and the desired number of sentences. After clicking submit, you will be directed to a new page that shows the input and output text.

Algorithms


Distance: Selects the longest sentence in the text. If multiple sentences are requested it will display in descending order of length.

Bag of words: Uses histograms to find the sentences that best resemble the overall text

Transformers: Transformer models are advanced deep learning architectures that employ self-attention mechanisms to process and understand vast amounts of textual data. These models are pre-trained on massive corpora, enabling them to generate summaries and perform various other natural language processing tasks with high accuracy and coherence. All of our 11 models have been fine-tuned for specific genres of text.

Pegasus: Based on the Transformer architecture that leverages self-supervised learning to create abstractive summaries by focusing on salient information extraction and text generation.

T5 Model: Versatile model that frames all NLP tasks as text-to-text problems, with summarization being one of its prominent applications

BART Model: Denoising autoencoder model that utilizes a bidirectional Transformer architecture for sequence-to-sequence tasks, excelling in text summarization through reconstruction and generation of coherent summaries.
